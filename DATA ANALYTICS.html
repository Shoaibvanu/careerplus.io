DATA ANALYTICS

ABOUT THE COURSE

The institute provides comprehensive Data Science and Analytics courses to equip students with skills in data analysis, visualization, and cutting-edge techniques. Topics covered include data analysis tools like Pandas, data visualization with Matplotlib and Seaborn, machine learning with TensorFlow and PyTorch, big data technologies like Hadoop and Spark, databases including SQL and NoSQL. The courses range from 6-14 weeks depending on the complexity.
Industry experts with strong training backgrounds deliver high-quality data science courses. Students can opt for in-person or virtual sessions based on their preferences. The courses focus on real-world applications, hands-on skills, and preparing students for industry demands. Key areas covered include data analysis, advanced machine learning techniques, big data tools, and database technologies. Overall, the curriculum aims to provide students with practical abilities in data science and analytics to succeed in the field.
