About our company

THE COMPANY

Career Plus is a new venture that commenced in 2024, focusing on providing online training and certification programs for top IT industry skills. Our certification and training programs span a variety of domains, including Cloud, Project Management, Big Data, Data Science & AI, Prompt Engineering, MuleSoft, DevOps, RPA, Design Thinking, Full Stack Development, Scrum Alliance, Generative AI, Data Analytics, and many other cutting-edge technologies. With a 24*7 dedicated support facility and competitive pricing, we assist professionals in making informed career selections. At Career Plus, we carefully choose trainers with over a decade of industry experience, offering extensive hands-on projects, closely monitoring student progress, and providing industry-recognized certifications. Our track record includes successfully conducting 32+ hours of training and certification programs for upcoming IT professionals, with many of them securing positions in top companies.
Career Plus is proud to offer internships to all learners who have successfully completed the training. Additionally, we provide full/partial credits (waive-offs) for authorized certification exams, offering financial aid based on eligibility in mock tests. We aim to instill confidence in our students to excel in certification exams. Alongside our comprehensive training programs, we conduct a 2-week crash course for authorized certification exams.
