ABOUT THE COURSES

The institute provides comprehensive programming courses covering a wide range of languages and tools, including Python, Java, SQL, PySpark, MySQL, PostgreSQL, and Jinja SQL. These courses are designed to equip learners with the skills needed for software development, data analysis, and database management.
The programming courses have varying durations, typically ranging from 8 to 14 weeks, depending on the complexity of the language or tool. Taught by industry experts with strong training backgrounds, the courses offer flexibility with options for in-person or virtual sessions. Emphasizing real-world skills and hands-on learning, the curriculum prepares students to meet the demands of the ever-evolving tech industry.
