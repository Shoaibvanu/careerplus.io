ABOUT THE COURSE

The institute offers comprehensive Project Management courses covering topics like Agile, Scrum, project management tools, and IT service management. The Agile and Scrum course provides training in these popular development methodologies. The Project Management Tools course covers tools like Jira and Trello for managing technology projects. The IT Service Management course focuses on frameworks like ITIL.
The Project Management courses range from 6-12 weeks depending on the topic complexity. Industry experts with strong training backgrounds deliver the high-quality courses. Students can choose between in-person and virtual sessions based on their preferences. The courses emphasize real-world applications, hands-on skills, and preparing students for the demands of the industry job market. Key topics covered include Agile, Scrum, project management tools, and IT service management frameworks. Overall, the curriculum aims to equip students with methodologies and tools required to lead successful technology projects.
