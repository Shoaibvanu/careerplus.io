UI-UX DESIGN

ABOUT THE COURSE

The institute provides comprehensive UI/UX Design courses covering topics like UI design principles, UX design, and prototyping tools. The UI design course focuses on interface design principles and best practices. The UX design course is about optimizing the user experience and flows. The prototyping tools course covers tools like Sketch, Figma, and Adobe XD.
The UI/UX Design courses range from 6-12 weeks depending on the topic complexity. Industry experts with strong training backgrounds deliver the high-quality courses. Students can opt for in-person or virtual sessions based on their preferences. The courses emphasize real-world applications, hands-on skills, and preparing students for industry demands. Key topics covered include UI/UX principles, processes, and leading prototyping tools. Overall, the curriculum aims to equip students with the skills to create seamless, user-friendly designs and experiences.
